Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka is a beautiful island country located in South Asia. It is known for its rich culture, beautiful beaches, ancient temples, and green mountains. The capital city of Sri Lanka is Sri Jayawardenepura Kotte, while Colombo is the commercial capital. Sri Lanka is famous for tea production, wildlife, and tourist attractions such as Sigiriya, Ella, and Galle Fort. People in Sri Lanka mainly speak Sinhala and Tamil. It is often called the "Pearl of the Indian Ocean.
